Admissions and Registration

We welcome applications from learners ready to commit to our values of discipline and excellence. Follow the steps below and prepare the required documents before your registration date.

Registration Timeline

  1. August 2026

    Applications open

    Collect or download the application form and begin gathering documents.

  2. September 2026

    Submit application

    Hand in completed forms with all supporting documents at the front office.

  3. October 2026

    Placement confirmation

    Successful applicants are notified and invited to an orientation session.

  4. January 2027

    First day of school

    New learners begin the academic year. Welcome to Qhamuka!

Required Documents

  • ✓

    Certified copy of learner's birth certificate

    Must be certified within the last three months.

  • ✓

    Most recent school report

    From the learner's current or previous school.

  • ✓

    Parent / guardian ID copies

    Certified copies of both parents or the legal guardian.

  • ✓

    Proof of residence

    A recent utility bill or affidavit confirming address.

  • ✓

    Immunisation / clinic card

    Where applicable for the learner's records.

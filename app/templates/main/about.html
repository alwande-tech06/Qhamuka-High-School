Our History

Established to serve the educational needs of the Somopho Reserve community, Qhamuka High School has steadily expanded its facilities and programmes. From modest beginnings, the school has cultivated a reputation for discipline, perseverance and pride in its learners' achievements.

Our Mission

To provide quality, accessible education that equips every learner with the knowledge, character and confidence to contribute meaningfully to their communities and the nation - always guided by our motto, "Work towards Excellence."

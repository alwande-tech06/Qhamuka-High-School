Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

Qhamuka High School ("the School") is a public secondary school located at Somopho Reserve, Empangeni, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. The School is the responsible party for personal information collected through this website, in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only the personal information necessary to process enquiries and application requests. This includes:

  • Parent or guardian full name, email address, and phone number
  • Learner first name, surname, current grade, and grade applying for
  • Messages submitted through the contact form

We do not collect ID numbers, financial information, medical records, or any other sensitive personal information through this website. Physical documentation is handled in person at the school office.

3. Why We Collect It

Personal information is collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To process application requests and contact the relevant parent or guardian
  • To respond to enquiries submitted through the contact form
  • To communicate administrative information about the admissions process

4. How Long We Keep It

Contact form submissions and application requests are retained for no longer than 12 months from the date of submission, unless the application proceeds to formal enrolment, in which case records are retained in accordance with the school's standard records management policy.

5. Who Has Access

Personal information submitted through this website is accessible only to authorised school administration staff. It is not shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third parties without your prior consent, except where required by law.

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to the processing of your personal information

7. Data Deletion Requests

To request that your personal information be removed from our records, please contact the school office directly:

  • Email: info@qhamukahigh.co.za
  • Phone: +27 71 657 5353
  • In person: Somopho Reserve, Empangeni, KZN — Mon–Fri 07:30–15:30

We will respond to deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics scripts. The Google Maps embed on the Contact page is served by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

9. Contact for Privacy Enquiries

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact the school office at info@qhamukahigh.co.za.
